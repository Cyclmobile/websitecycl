    Cycl Partner/Get started
    Partner onboarding

    How to succeed as a Cycl partner

    Use this guide as your operating checklist: complete your profile, publish offers customers actually want, upload redemption codes, and boost the rewards that drive traffic in your area.

    01

    Complete your profile

    Open Profile and add your company name, contact details, business category, city, and website. Accurate info helps users trust your offers and helps us match you to the right stations.

    02

    Publish your first reward

    Go to Rewards and add an offer with a clear title, image, Cycl Coin cost, and the locations where it can be redeemed. Strong everyday offers (coffee, lunch, student deals) outperform vague ads.

    03

    Upload redemption codes

    Add single codes or bulk-upload a list. Codes let you measure real customer visits and bill on validated value rather than visibility alone.

    04

    Boost what's working

    Use Sponsored Popular placements for launches, weekends, or when a nearby station gets high traffic. Boosts pay back fastest on rewards that already convert.

    05

    Track performance

    Check Analytics for clicks, activations, conversion rate, and city spread. Keep your best offers visible and replace weak ones quickly.

    06

    Manage billing

    Add a payment method in Billing, choose a plan that fits your station coverage, and pay any outstanding boosts so campaigns keep running without interruption.

    Practical advice

    What usually drives success.

    Start with one strong offer.Coffee, lunch, student discounts, and everyday rewards usually perform better than vague brand ads.
    Use sponsored placement when timing matters.Sponsored Popular works best for launches, weekend campaigns, or stations with high traffic.
    Upload codes in bulk.Codes let you measure real visits and bill on validated value, not just visibility.
    Request collection quickly.If a station at your place is full and the bag has been changed, request pickup from Assigned stations.
