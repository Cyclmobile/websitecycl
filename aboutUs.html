We are building a cleaner return loop.

Cycl is a company that turns bottle and can recycling into a simple mobile experience. We combine Cycl stations, AI recognition, partner rewards, and analytics tools so more containers make it back into useful material streams while businesses learn more about customer behavior.

01 Why we exist

Recycling should feel like a normal part of everyday movement, not a separate chore.

Traditional deposit systems work well where expensive machines are installed, but many places never get that infrastructure. Cycl starts from the opposite direction. We use the phone people already carry, Cycl stations that fit real spaces, and software that connects every return to rewards, customer insight, and better business decisions.

Our work is practical. We care about streets, schools, libraries, malls, cafés, campuses, and venues where people already finish drinks. The goal is to make a return point fit naturally into those places, then make every return visible, useful, and rewarding.

02 How we think

The best climate tools are easy to place, easy to use, and easy to trust.

Cycl is built around a few operating principles that keep the product grounded as it grows from pilots to larger networks.

01 / Access

Put returns where people are

Return points should be close to daily routines. More convenient locations create more returns and cleaner data.

02 / Simplicity

Keep hardware simple

Cycl stations do not need to be complicated. The intelligence can live in the app and platform.

03 / Proof

Make performance measurable

Every return should create a record that can support operations, partner value, and better business decisions.

03 Our path

From idea to network, one station at a time.

Cycl grows by testing in real places, learning from businesses and users, then turning those learnings into a stronger platform.
