WHO WE ARE

Cycl is a mobile app revolutionizing bottle recycling. Our mission is to make recycling as effortless as disposing of a soda bottle. We're achieving this by making recycling more accessible and available 24/7. With plastic pollution being one of today's greatest challenges, our vision is to create a world where we can use plastic bottles without worrying about their environmental impact.
