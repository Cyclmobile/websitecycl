01 Working at Cycl

You work close to the product, the users, and the real world.

Cycl is not only software on a screen. It lives in shops, libraries, malls, schools, and streets where people return bottles and cans. That means our team thinks about the whole system, from a camera flow in the app to the experience of placing a return station in a busy location.

We value people who can move between detail and outcome. Sometimes the right answer is a better interface. Sometimes it is a clearer partner process. Sometimes it is standing next to a station and watching where people hesitate.

02 How we work

Direct, curious, and serious about execution.

We are building something that has to work outside a presentation deck. That shapes the way we make decisions.
